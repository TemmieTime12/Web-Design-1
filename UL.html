Brownie in a cup

Ingrediants

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil (one that's not too strong is better)
  • 11/2 tablespoon water at room temp
  • 1 egg

Steps

  • Sift together sugar & flower
  • Add salt
  • Mix together the olive oil, water and egg
  • Fold the dry ingrediants into the wet
  • Pour into a mug and wait for 2 minutes
